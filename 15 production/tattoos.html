Permanent Expressions

Lately, Iâ€™ve been working a lot with portraiture and exploring both what a portrait is and how individuals use facial expressions to convey themselves. Other works of mine have hinted at the idea that we express ourselves in many ways and show many facets of our personalities. This work furthers that exploration by looking at a very different, and very permanent way, that people choose to express themselves. Historically, tattoos have been used in religion, as punishment, to show status or accomplishments, and have served many other purposes. Today, tattoos have become an art form, and while many would still consider them controversial, to the millennial generation especially, they have become a form of self-expression.
